How does this work?

  1. Get yourself some Spotify API credentials(see Register You App)
  2. Generate a token with playlist-read-collaborative scope using the Spotify testing app. Yup, it would be better if this step was part of this here app. I'll get to it soon.
  3. Add the token, the playlist ID for a playlist that has 30-50 songs, the number of cards your'd like to generate below and click Generate cards.
  4. Select all the cards, copy and paste into a Google Doc. They will magically appear formatted as tables in the doc, but you'll need to do a bit of cleanup to center the text and add page breaks between cards.
  5. Share the Doc with your friends
  6. Players claim a card by adding their name next to the card number
  7. Start a video call and play the Spotify playlist on shuffle. Works well with Zoom, since Zoom allows sharing computer audio directly to the meeting. The host should use 1 device to play the audio and join the video call on another device in a separate room.
  8. Players fill in their card as songs are played, while enjoying festive food and beverage.
  9. When a player fills in a row, they should shout "Rock n roll!" and award themselves a prize of some sort (some kind of useless junk lying around the house).
